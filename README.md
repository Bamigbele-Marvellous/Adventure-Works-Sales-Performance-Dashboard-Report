### Project Overview
This project analyzes sales performance for Adventure Works, focusing on revenue generation, profit margins, transaction volume, and sales trends across time and locations. The objective is to identify key drivers of profitability and understand sales patterns.
### Data Sources
The dataset includes sales transaction records containing:
- Product sales quantity
- Revenue
- Cost of goods sold (COGS)
- Profit and profit margins
- Sales transactions
- Time-based sales trends
- Country-level sales performance
These variables enable a detailed evaluation of sales performance.
### Tools used
The dashboard and analysis were created using:
- Power BI
- Data visualization techniques
- KPI indicators for revenue and profit tracking
### Data Cleaning and Preparation
The dataset was prepared by performing the following steps:
- Verified transaction records for missing or incorrect values
- Standardized date and time formats
- Aggregated sales revenue and cost metrics
- Calculated profit margins and total profits
- Organized data by time period and geographic region
### Exploratory Data Analysis
Key insights from the dashboard include:
- Total sales quantity reached 631,920 units.
- Total revenue is $307.09M, while total profit is $126.29M.
- The company maintains a strong profit margin of 41.12%.
- Weekday sales account for approximately 72% of total profit, indicating stronger weekday demand.
- Quarter 4 generated the highest profits, suggesting seasonal sales peaks.
### Results
The analysis shows strong financial performance with high profitability and consistent revenue growth. Sales patterns indicate clear seasonal trends and stronger demand during weekdays.
### Recommendations
- Increase marketing and promotional efforts during high-performing quarters such as Q4.
- Develop strategies to improve weekend sales performance.
- Expand sales efforts in regions with strong revenue potential.
